# Risk Curveball Challenge
## Scenario
Your team has been working on the Derm-Assist MVP: a tool to help GPs triage skin lesion images. Suddenly, new information arrives; your project environment has changed.

**Your job: adapt your MVP project board to reflect the new constraint.**
1. Assess the impact on your MoSCoW priorities and user stories.
2. Update your Project Board.
3. Prepare a 3-minute summary explaining what you changed and why.
